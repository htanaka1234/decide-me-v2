# Output Contract

Question turns must include:

- `Decision:`
- `Proposal:`
- `Question:`
- `Recommendation:`
- `Why:`
- `If not:`

Acceptance turns must include:

- `Accepted: D-...` or `Accepted: B-...`

Close-session turns must include:

- `Closed: S-...`
- the generated close summary

Plan-generation turns must include one of:

- `Conflicts:`
- `Action Plan:`
